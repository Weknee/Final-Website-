Chicken Adobo

420 kcal • Category: Main

Savory soy-vinegar braised chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, garlic, and bay leaves. Marinate for 30 minutes (optional).
  2. Sear chicken in a pan until lightly browned; add marinade, vinegar, and peppercorns.
  3. Bring to a boil, then simmer 20–30 minutes until chicken is cooked and sauce reduces. Adjust seasoning.

Notes / Tips

You can add coconut milk for a creamier adobo (Adobo sa Gata). Serve with rice.
